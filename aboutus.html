Our Story

The journey of Pariksha Parikrama began with a vision to transform competitive exam preparation

Founded in 2022 by Dr. Jeevith N., Pariksha Parikrama emerged from a simple yet powerful observation: traditional coaching methods were failing to address the unique needs and psychological challenges faced by competitive exam aspirants.

The name "Pariksha Parikrama" symbolizes the sacred journey of preparation - a holistic approach that combines academic rigor with psychological well-being, strategic planning with personalized mentorship.

What started as a small initiative with a handful of students has now transformed into a trusted institution that has guided hundreds of aspirants toward their dreams of qualifying for prestigious exams like KSET, UGC-NET, and various lecturer recruitment exams.

Why We're Different

  • Personalized mentorship tailored to individual learning styles
  • Integration of psychological principles with academic preparation
  • Focus on building confidence alongside conceptual clarity
  • Proven strategies that make competitive exams less competitive
