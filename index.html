Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What is the duration of your courses?
Our courses typically range from 1 to 3 months, depending on the exam and program. KSET preparation is 2 months, UGC-NET is 1 months, and Lecturers' Exam preparation is 4 months.
Do you provide recorded sessions?
No, live sessions are not recorded .
Is there any doubt-solving support?
Absolutely! We provide 12/7 doubt-solving support through our dedicated platform. You can also schedule one-on-one sessions with mentors for personalized guidance.
Do you offer demo classes?
Yes! We offer a free demo class for all our courses after registration. This allows you to experience our teaching methodology before making a commitment.
